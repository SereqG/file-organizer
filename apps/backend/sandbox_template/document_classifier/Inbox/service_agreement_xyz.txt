SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is made effective as of May 1, 2024,
between CloudHost Pro LLC ("Service Provider") and MegaRetail Corp ("Client").

1. SERVICES
Service Provider agrees to provide the following services to Client:
- Cloud infrastructure management and monitoring
- 24/7 technical support (SLA: 99.9% uptime guarantee)
- Monthly security audits and vulnerability assessments
- Automated backups and disaster recovery planning

2. COMPENSATION
Client agrees to pay Service Provider $4,500 per month, invoiced on the 1st
of each month. Payment is due within 15 days of invoice date.

3. TERM
This Agreement commences on May 1, 2024, and continues for twelve (12) months,
automatically renewing unless terminated with 30 days written notice.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree to maintain the confidentiality of proprietary information
shared during the course of this engagement.

5. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY
Service Provider's liability shall not exceed the fees paid in the preceding
three months.
