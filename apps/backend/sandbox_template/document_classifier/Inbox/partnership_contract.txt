PARTNERSHIP CONTRACT

This Partnership Contract ("Contract") is entered into on June 15, 2024,
between Alpha Ventures ("Partner A") and Beta Innovations ("Partner B"),
collectively referred to as "the Partners."

1. PURPOSE
The Partners agree to collaborate on the development and commercialization of
a joint AI-powered data analytics platform ("the Product").

2. CONTRIBUTIONS
Partner A contributes: Technology IP, development resources (5 engineers),
and initial seed funding of $200,000.
Partner B contributes: Market access, sales network, and domain expertise.

3. PROFIT SHARING
Net profits shall be distributed: 55% to Partner A, 45% to Partner B.
Losses shall be shared in the same proportion.

4. DECISION MAKING
Major decisions require unanimous consent. Day-to-day operations are managed
by a jointly appointed Project Director.

5. DURATION
This Partnership is effective for 24 months, with option to extend by mutual
written agreement.

6. DISSOLUTION
Either party may dissolve the Partnership with 60 days written notice.
IP developed jointly remains co-owned upon dissolution.
