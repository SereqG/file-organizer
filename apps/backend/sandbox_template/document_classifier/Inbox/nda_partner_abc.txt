NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of April 1, 2024,
by and between ABC Technologies Inc. ("Disclosing Party") and XYZ Solutions Ltd.
("Receiving Party").

1. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
The Receiving Party agrees to hold in strict confidence and not to disclose to
any third party any Confidential Information disclosed by the Disclosing Party.
"Confidential Information" means any data or information that is proprietary to
the Disclosing Party and not generally known to the public.

2. OBLIGATIONS
The Receiving Party shall: (a) protect the Confidential Information using the same
degree of care it uses to protect its own confidential information; (b) not use
the Confidential Information for any purpose other than evaluating a potential
business relationship; (c) limit access to personnel who have a need to know.

3. TERM
This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of three (3) years from the
date of execution.

4. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
